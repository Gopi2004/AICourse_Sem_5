AI stands for "Artificial Intelligence." It refers to the simulation of human intelligence in machines or computer systems. AI technologies enable machines to perform tasks that typically require human intelligence, such as problem-solving, learning, understanding natural language, and recognizing patterns. AI encompasses various subfields, including machine learning, deep learning, natural language processing, computer vision, and robotics. AI has a wide range of applications in fields like healthcare, finance, transportation, and many others, and it continues to advance and evolve rapidly.
